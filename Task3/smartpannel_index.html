About the Data That We Collect

Privacy

The Smart App runs in the background while anonymously and securely collecting data about how you use those devices. We collect data about the usage of devices, device features, apps, websites, and services. To learn more, see our Privacy Policy.

How Is the Data Used?

The research data collected in the Smart Panel is used to help your favorite apps, websites, and technology companies improve their services and features for everyone. We do not share any personal or protected information, such as credit card information or passwords, with third parties.

About Smart Panel

The Smart Panel is a market research project run by Verto Analytics with the goal of making all your favorite technology even better. Smart Panel members earn rewards by anonymously sharing data about how they use their devices. That data is then analyzed to help companies understand how to give you a better online experience.
